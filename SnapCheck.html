The Indus Valley Civilization

(also known as The Harappan Civilization)

One of the earliest civilizations in the world. Mohenjo-daro is the largest site and Allahdino is the smallest site of the Indus Valley Civilization. Most of the houses were two-storey and very spacious. Towns were laid down in a rectangular pattern.

There were well built granaries, burial grounds and bathing platforms. At the time of excavation many bronze, copper and pottery products along with terra-cottatoys have been excavated.
